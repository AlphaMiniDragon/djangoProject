What are recipe guides and what do they teach?

We say “cookbook” and we mean “recipe book” with step-by-step instructions, precise recommendations and vivid photographs. Whole tomes about fish, meat, vegetarian dishes, desserts and drinks ... But there are other publications - those in which the number of recipes is minimized or none at all. They teach the basics of cooking, help you discover new combinations of tastes, explain the intricacies of cooking and simply inspire you to create gastronomic masterpieces in the most ordinary kitchen. We will tell you how to use them to discover the chef in yourself.
